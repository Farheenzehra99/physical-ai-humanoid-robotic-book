# URDF Designer Skill

**Skill ID**: `urdf_designer`
**Category**: Simulation
**Complexity**: Medium
**Introduced**: Week 2

## Overview

Generate and validate URDF/Xacro files for humanoid robots with configurable DOF, kinematics, and visual properties.

## Capabilities

- Generate 18-DOF humanoid URDF from templates
- Validate URDF syntax and kinematics
- Convert between URDF and Xacro formats
- Export to Isaac Sim USD format
